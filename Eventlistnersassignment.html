What is the coldplay ?

Throughout their career, Coldplay has been known for their elaborate live performances, often incorporating stunning visuals and stage effects. They have won numerous awards, including multiple Grammys, and have sold over 100 million albums worldwide, making them one of the best-selling music acts in history.

What is te Football ?

Football, also known as association football or soccer, is a team sport where two teams of 11 players compete to score goals by moving the ball into the other team's goal. The game is played on a rectangular field, called a pitch, and players use their feet to propel the ball. The team with the most goals at the end of the game wins.
